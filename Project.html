About

Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIIT),Kottayam is a premier institute of higher education in the field of Information Technology located in Kottayam, Kerala, India. It was established in the year 2015 under the mentorship of National Institute of Technology,Calicut (which later changed to IISER, Thiruvananthpuram in 2016) and is one of the 20 IIITs established under the Public-Private Partnership (PPP) model by the Ministry of Education, Government of India.
The institute offers undergraduate and postgraduate degree programs in various branches of Information Technology such as Computer Science and Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, and Computational Data Science. The curriculum is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in the principles and practices of information technology, as well as opportunities to engage in cutting-edge research and development.


Campus

The campus of Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIIT) Kottayam is located in Valavoor village in Pala, Kottayam, Kerala. Spread over an area of 53 acres, the campus is set amidst lush green surroundings, providing a peaceful and conducive environment for learning and research.
The campus is well-equipped with modern infrastructure and facilities to cater to the needs of students and faculty. The academic block houses classrooms, tutorial rooms, laboratories, and research centers equipped with state-of-the-art technology. The library is well-stocked with books, journals, and e-resources to support research and academic activities.
The institute also provides hostel facilities for both boys and girls, with well-furnished rooms, Wi-Fi connectivity, and recreational facilities. The hostels have round-the-clock security for the well-being of the students.

Courses Provided
IIIT Kottayam provides admission in following courses:
  1. Under Graduate Programmes
    • B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)
    • B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)
    • B.Tech in Computer Science with specialisation in Cyber Security
    • B.Tech in Computer Science with specialisation in AI & Data Science

  2. Post Graduate Programmes (For Working Professionals)
    • M.Tech in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science
    • M.Tech in Cyber Security
    • M.Tech in CSE with specialization in FinTech
    • M.Tech. in CSE with specialization in Big Data and Machine Learning

  3. Doctoral Programmes
    • Computer Science and Engineering(CSE)
    • Electronics and Communication Engineering(ECE)
    • Mathematics
